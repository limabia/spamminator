Repair MBR in Windows to Prevent Boot Issues
The MBR (Master Boot Record) is a crucial part of your PC’s startup system. If it is damaged, you won’t be able to boot into Windows and operate your computer. EaseUS Partition Master can help you repair the damaged MBR and get your PC to start properly without reinstalling the OS in simple clicks.

Move Windows installation to New Hard Drive or SSD
A good way to boost PC's performance is to upgrade to a larger HDD or a faster SSD. In doing so, however, you’ll have to move your operating system (and all the data it holds) over to the new drive. This can be a risky task and shouldn't be performed without knowing exactly what to do. Here, we run you though how to move Windows 10 to a new HDD/SSD without data loss.
